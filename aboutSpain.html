    Best to worst times to visit Spain

    1. Spring
      • Flowers are out
      • Not too hot
      • Holy Week Processions in the South
    2. Fall
      • Not too hot
      • Leaves turn orange in Madrid
      • Not as pretty as spring
    3. Winter
      • Freezing cold in Madrid!
      • Not very cold in the South
      • Less crowded than in other seasons
    4. Summer
      • EXTREMELY hot, everywhere
      • Very crowded tourist-wise
      • A good opportunity to visit the beautiful coast
